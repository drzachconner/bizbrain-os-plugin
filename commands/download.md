---
name: download
description: Download videos, audio, courses from any platform. Specialized Skool classroom bulk support.
argument-hint: <url or "skool bulk">
---

Invoke the `bizbrain-os:media-downloader` skill and follow it.

- `/download <url>` — Download video/audio from URL
- `/download skool` — Guided Skool classroom bulk download
- `/download playlist <url>` — Download entire playlist/channel
- `/download audio <url>` — Extract audio only (MP3)
- `/download batch <file>` — Download all URLs from a text file
- `/download update` — Update yt-dlp and gallery-dl to latest

**Arguments:** $ARGUMENTS
